

GETTING TO THE NEW HLC SITE
===========================
Type the following address in your browser (PC, tablet or phone):  hlcweb.azurewebsites.net

On your tablet or phone, when the logon page appears do the following to add an icon to your home screen:

  iPad or iPhone:
  - Tap the Send icon (the square with an arrow pointing up) then tap "Add to Home Screen" icon

  Android:
  - Press the Menu button (either below the screen on some devices or at the top-right corner of the browser) , then tap on Page
  - Tap Add to Home Screen
